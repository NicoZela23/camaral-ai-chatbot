# Technical FAQ

## General Questions

### What technology powers Camaral avatars?
Camaral uses advanced AI including large language models, natural language processing, computer vision, and neural voice synthesis.

### How accurate are the avatars?
Our avatars achieve >95% accuracy in understanding and responding to queries when properly configured with comprehensive knowledge bases.

### What languages are supported?
Camaral supports 50+ languages with native fluency, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and more.

### How fast are responses?
Response latency is typically under 2 seconds, ensuring natural conversation flow.
