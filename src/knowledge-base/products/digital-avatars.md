# Digital Avatar Technology

## How Camaral's Digital Avatars Work

Camaral's digital avatars are sophisticated AI systems that combine multiple cutting-edge technologies to create realistic, intelligent virtual meeting participants.

## Core Technologies

### 1. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
Our NLP engine enables avatars to:
- Understand complex questions and requests
- Interpret context from conversation history
- Detect emotional tone and sentiment
- Generate natural, contextually appropriate responses
- Support 50+ languages with native fluency

### 2. Computer Vision & Animation
Realistic visual presentation through:
- Photorealistic 3D rendering
- Real-time facial expressions
- Natural gesture generation
- Eye contact and gaze tracking
- Lip-sync accuracy >98%

### 3. Voice Synthesis
Natural-sounding speech featuring:
- Neural text-to-speech technology
- Emotion-aware voice modulation
- Multiple voice options and accents
- Custom voice cloning available
- Real-time speech generation

### 4. Conversation Management
Intelligent dialogue handling:
- Multi-turn conversation tracking
- Topic switching and recovery
- Interruption handling
- Clarification requests
- Knowledge retrieval from company databases
