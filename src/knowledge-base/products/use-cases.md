# Use Cases & Applications

## Sales Use Cases

### Lead Qualification
Automatically qualify inbound leads 24/7:
- Ask qualifying questions based on lead profile
- Score leads based on responses
- Route high-value leads to sales team
- Provide instant responses to prospects

### Product Demonstrations
Conduct personalized demos at scale:
- Customize demo based on prospect needs
- Answer questions in real-time
- Show relevant features and benefits
- Schedule follow-up meetings automatically

### Sales Follow-ups
Never miss a follow-up opportunity:
- Automated follow-up scheduling
- Personalized follow-up messages
- Reminder notifications
- Progress tracking
